Idukki Dam

24x7

The Idukki Dam is a double curvature Arch dam constructed across the Periyar River in a narrow gorge between two granite hills locally known as Kuravan and Kurathi. At 168.91 metres, it is one of the highest arch dams in Asia

Meesapulimala

24x7

Meesapulimala is an Indian peak, the next south of the second highest peak of the Western Ghats on the Idukki District and Theni District border. Its peak is 2,640 metres above sea level. The name derives from its appearance from the southwest of a tiger with prominent whiskers

Cheeyappara Waterfalls

24x7

The Cheeyappara Waterfall is on the Kochi - Madurai Highway, between Neriamangalam and Adimali. The Cheeyappara Waterfall cascades down in seven steps. This is also a great place for trekking.There is a dense growth of trees all around and several rare species of plants and animals can be seen in the vicinity.
